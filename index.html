Brian J. Knaus

Quantitative and computational biologist, evolutionary genomics

A biologist, these days, must wear many hats. Author, naturalist, molecular and quantitative geneticist, computational biologist, statistician and graphic artist. We run the risk of becoming a jack of all trades but master of none. Through this process we learn what issues are important, and we learn to master these. A biologist, these days, is a jack of many trades, as well as a master of several well chosen trades.
